The impossible national park

El Imposible National Park is a national park in El Salvador. It was created on January 1, 1989 and covers an area of 5,000 hectares. It has an altitude between 250 and 1,425 meters. In 1992 El Imposible entered UNESCO's provisional lists of World Heritage Sites along with Cara Sucia
